Ingredients

  • 4 cups shaved ice
  • 1/2 cup sweetened red beans (monggo)
  • 1/2 cup sweetened white beans
  • 1/2 cup nata de coco (coconut gel)
  • 1/2 cup kaong (sugar palm fruit)
  • 1/2 cup sago (tapioca pearls), cooked
  • 1/2 cup sweetened jackfruit (langka), sliced
  • 1/2 cup ube halaya (purple yam jam)
  • 4 slices leche flan (caramel custard)
  • 4 scoops ube ice cream (or vanilla)
  • 1/4 cup evaporated milk
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare all the ingredients. You can use store-bought sweetened preserves or make your own.
  2. In each serving glass, start by adding a layer of sweetened red beans at the bottom.
  3. Add white beans, nata de coco, kaong, sago, and jackfruit in layers.
  4. Add a spoonful of ube halaya on top of the beans and fruits.
  5. Fill the glass with shaved ice, packing it down firmly.
  6. Drizzle evaporated milk over the shaved ice. Sprinkle with sugar if desired.
  7. Top with a slice of leche flan and a scoop of ube ice cream.
  8. Serve immediately with a long spoon. Mix everything together before eating - that's why it's called "halo-halo" (mix-mix)!
