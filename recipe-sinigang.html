Sinigang na Baboy

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork ribs or pork belly, cut into pieces
  • 1 packet sinigang mix (tamarind soup base)
  • 1 large onion, quartered
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 radish (labanos), sliced
  • 1 bundle string beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 bundle water spinach (kangkong) or bok choy
  • 2-3 pieces long green chili peppers (siling haba)
  • 8-10 cups water
  • Fish sauce (patis) to taste
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add the pork pieces and cook for 30-40 minutes, or until tender. Skim off any scum that rises to the surface.
  2. Add the onion and tomatoes. Simmer for 5 minutes until tomatoes are soft.
  3. Add the sinigang mix and stir until dissolved. Let it simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add the radish and cook for 3-5 minutes until slightly tender.
  5. Add the string beans and green chili peppers. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add the water spinach or bok choy last, as it cooks quickly. Simmer for 1-2 minutes until just wilted.
  7. Season with fish sauce and salt according to your taste. The soup should be tangy and savory.
  8. Serve hot over steamed rice. Enjoy this comforting Filipino sour soup!
