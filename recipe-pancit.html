Pancit Bihon

Ingredients

  • 1 pack (8 oz) rice noodles (bihon)
  • 1/2 lb chicken breast, sliced
  • 1/2 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup carrots, julienned
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1 cup celery, sliced
  • 1/2 cup green beans, sliced
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Calamansi or lemon wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Soak the rice noodles in warm water for 10-15 minutes until soft but still firm. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large wok or pan over medium-high heat. Saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add chicken and cook until no longer pink, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add shrimp and cook until pink, about 2-3 minutes. Remove chicken and shrimp from the pan and set aside.
  5. In the same pan, add carrots, celery, and green beans. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add cabbage and cook for 1 minute until slightly wilted.
  7. Push vegetables to one side of the pan. Add the drained rice noodles and pour in the chicken stock.
  8. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and fish sauce. Toss everything together until noodles are well coated.
  9. Return the chicken and shrimp to the pan. Mix everything together and cook for 3-5 minutes until noodles are tender and most of the liquid is absorbed.
  10. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with calamansi or lemon wedges on the side. Enjoy your delicious Pancit Bihon!
