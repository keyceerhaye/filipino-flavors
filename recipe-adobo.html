Chicken Adobo

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken thighs or drumsticks
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1 head of garlic, cloves crushed
  • 2-3 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon whole black peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine soy sauce, vinegar, crushed garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns. Add the chicken pieces and marinate for at least 30 minutes (or overnight for best flavor).
  2. Heat oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Remove chicken from marinade (reserve the marinade) and brown the chicken pieces on all sides, about 5 minutes per side.
  3. Pour the reserved marinade over the chicken, then add water and brown sugar. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and cover.
  4. Simmer for 25-30 minutes, or until chicken is tender and cooked through. Turn the chicken pieces occasionally to ensure even cooking.
  5. Remove the lid and continue cooking until the sauce has reduced and thickened slightly, about 5-10 more minutes.
  6. Remove bay leaves and adjust seasoning with salt if needed. The sauce should be savory, tangy, and slightly sweet.
  7. Serve hot over steamed rice. Enjoy this classic Filipino comfort food!
